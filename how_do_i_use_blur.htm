How to Use Blur Correctly
(Read this at least 10 times.)

It appears that I can't repeat this often enough, so here goes...

There are several things you need to remember about using blur:

1. Always use it.
Signs are just not gonna look good without it.

2. Don't screw up the tags
Don't use \blur.5 instead of \blur0.5.

3. Don't use blur0.3 or lower because it dosen't do anything.
You could actually see this if you looked at the screen, but apparently people don't look at what they're doing.

4. Always check your signs at 100%
Otherwise the signs get blurred by resizing and you can't see how much blur you need.

5. Learn how to do blur right for signs with border.
Read the Layers chapter in the guide. Read the Layers chapter in the guide. I'm saying it twice because you keep missing it.
